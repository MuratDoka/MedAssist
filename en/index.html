How many days should we stay in Turkey?

Depending on the type of treatment, patients coming to Turkey for medical tourism generally stay for three days, but if you have both hair transplantation and dental veneer treatments, this period can extend up to ten days.

Will I experience any transportation issues?

No, we provide transportation services with VIP vehicles to assist you until your treatment is successfully completed.

How does Hollywood Smile actually work?

Hollywood smile works similarly to most dental products. Our premium gels contain the same components that are used by dentists to whiten your teeth. In october, there are several other healthy components that promote whitening, help cleanse and protect your teeth. The gel holds your teeth long enough for the whitening agents to be absorbed, leaving you with a bright, safe and healthy smile with little or no sensitivity.

Is Hollywood Smile safe?

Yes! Our products work by removing stains from teeth. When used correctly, it does not damage the enamel. We also offer Remineralization gel, which strengthens tooth enamel after teeth whitening, allows the enamel to actively remineralize itself in the presence of fluoride, calcium and phosphate.

Are dental veneers permanent?

It is designed to stay in place for the rest of your life. They usually last between 10-20 years, and if one of your coatings decays, wears out, or breaks, you’ll need to replace it. You can never reverse the treatment.

Do you brush veneers?

All you have to do is brush and floss regularly. Avoiding foods and drinks that create stains, such as coffee, red wine, and dark fruits, can help ensure that your coatings don’t stain over time.
