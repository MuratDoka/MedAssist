About Us

Welcome to Serenity MedAssist - your premier destination for health tourism. We specialize in providing comprehensive healthcare solutions coupled with unforgettable travel experiences, ensuring our clients receive top-notch medical treatments while exploring captivating destinations in Albania and Turkey.

At Serenity MedAssist we understand that seeking medical care can be a daunting experience. That's why we've made it our mission to simplify the process by offering personalized assistance every step of the way. From initial consultation to post-treatment care, our dedicated team of professionals is committed to ensuring your comfort, safety, and satisfaction throughout your journey.

What sets us apart is our extensive network of internationally renowned hospitals, clinics, and healthcare professionals. Whether you're in need of cosmetic surgery, dental procedures, eye examination, fertility treatments, oncology diseases, organ transplantation, or other specialized medical care, we connect you with leading experts who deliver exceptional results using state-of-the-art technology and innovative techniques.

At Serenity MedAssist, your well-being is our priority. With our commitment to excellence, unparalleled customer service, and a passion for creating unforgettable experiences, we strive to redefine health tourism and empower you to reclaim your health and vitality with confidence and peace of mind.

Embark on a journey of healing, discovery, and transformation with Serenity MedAssist. Your path to wellness starts here.

Navigating Health Tourism with Ease: The Serenity MedAssist Experience

Your Journey, Our Commitment: Personalized Care Every Step of the Way

Call

Our knowledgeable and compassionate representatives warmly welcome you when you contact Serenity MedAssist. They gather essential information about your healthcare needs and preferences, provide an overview of our services, and listen attentively to your concerns and questions. We encourage open communication and welcome inquiries about our services, destinations, or treatment options.

Consideration

After the initial call, your health tourism journey with us enters the consideration phase, where you can expect

  • Personalized Consultation
  • Treatment Planning and Coordination
  • Financial Considerations
  • Support and Guidance

Flight

Our travel coordinators work closely with you to understand your travel preferences, including departure dates, airline preferences, and any specific accommodations or requirements. We provide personalized assistance to help plan your flight itinerary and make arrangements that best suit your needs.

Greet & Treatment

Upon arrival at your health tourism destination, the 'Greet' stage begins, ensuring a seamless and welcoming experience:

  • Personalized Welcome
  • Orientation and Information
  • Introduction to Healthcare Providers
  • Ongoing Support

Discover the country

As part of the 'Discovering the Country' stage, we provide an orientation session to familiarize you with the local culture, customs, and etiquette. We offer insights into the country's history, traditions, and lifestyle, helping you immerse yourself in the local environment.

Happy Back Home

During the 'Happy Back Home' stage, we support your transition back to daily life:

  • Post-Treatment Care
  • Follow-up Communication
  • Feedback and Evaluation
  • Continued Support
  • Celebration of Success
