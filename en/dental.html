About Hollywood Smile

In order to get a beautiful smile, just white teeth are not enough. In addition to whiteness, the healthy appearance of the gums and the proportion of the teeth are also very important. Hollywood Smile aims to achieve a perfect smile by ensuring gum, tooth, lip harmony.

With Hollywood smile design, a healthy and white smile is aimed as a result of correcting the person’s existing tooth structure with porcelain decking, eliminating the mismatch between the gums and teeth with gum aesthetics applications.

What procedures does Hollywood Smile include?

Hollywood smile is a treatment method that includes multiple cosmetic dental procedures aimed at improving the appearance of teeth. These procedures may include dental implants, dental veneers, teeth whitening, and reshaping of teeth. Dental implants are metal structures placed in the place of missing teeth, used to replace tooth loss. Dental veneers are thin coverings applied to the surface of teeth and are intended to improve the appearance of teeth. Teeth whitening is a procedure that aims to lighten the color of teeth and make them look brighter. Reshaping of teeth is a procedure that aims to change the shape of teeth and give them a more even appearance. Which of these procedures are performed depends on the condition of an individual’s teeth and the treatment plan is determined by the dentist. In a Hollywood smile treatment, one or more procedures may be applied and the goal is to achieve as natural-looking and beautiful a smile as possible.

What can the Hollywood Smile correct?

Hollywood smile is a cosmetic dental treatment method aimed at improving the appearance of teeth. These procedures may include dental implants, dental veneers, teeth whitening, and reshaping of teeth. These procedures can correct the following issues:

Hollywood smile works similarly to most dental products. Our premium gels contain the same components that are used by dentists to whiten your teeth. How long do the results last?

Lasting results usually depend on each person’s lifestyle and eating habits, but generally speaking, you should see results from 6 months to a year.
